Wordsearch Generator

Ever played a wordsearch? Why not customize your own in a minute?

The idea for this project came from work, after seeing a teacher painstakingly filling out every cell with a text-box, filling every text-box with a letter. I wanted to make something that would make the process easier and faster.

Features:

  • customizable words
  • customizable grid size
  • customizable title
  • language selection for letter inclusion
  • customizable directions
  • overlap toggle
  • solution toggle
  • product view
  • print
  • download

This was a fun project with some challenges with the grid layout and logic.
