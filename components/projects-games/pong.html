Pong

The famous pong game visualized in 3D.

Play against your friend(s) or an AI. Play either the classic 1v1 or a 4-player rumble. Play normal mode or get crazy with powerups. The choice is yours!

This was our final school group project, where we were supposed to create a website where people could log in and play against each other. I was mainly in charge of creating the actual game, using Three.js to create the stunning 3D world.

Instructions and download on this Github repo.
